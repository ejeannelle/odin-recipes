Vanilla Cake

Home

Description

This soft and buttery vanilla cake is a timeless treat. With a tender crumb and rich flavor, it’s ideal for birthdays, afternoon tea, or just because you deserve cake.

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour
  • 2½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1¾ cups (350g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) whole milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each. Stir in vanilla.
  5. Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk to the butter mixture, beginning and ending with dry.
  6. Divide batter evenly between pans and smooth the tops.
  7. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks.
  9. Frost once completely cool, using your favorite buttercream or whipped topping.
